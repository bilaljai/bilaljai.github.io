The Portfolio of Bilal Al-Jaibaji

Projects

Faithful recreation of the first level of NES Metroid, with the addition of a custom grappling hook mechanic that adds a new level of verticality!
  • Metroid (Unity Remaster)

My Contributions:

I primarily worked on the backbone of game with features such as camera movement, player logic, and the health system. Additionally, I

implemented the animations and interactions with the sandbox. I utilized Photoshop to customize sprites for the enemies and collectables.

I also implemented the AI for some of the enemies.

Technology Used:

Unity Game Engine to build level, Photoshop for sprites, Jira for project management
